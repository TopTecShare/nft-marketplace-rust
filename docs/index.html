Fouders Staves

The auction of these NFT staves is the first step of our schedule and will enable contacts with early investors and interested artists that want to collaborate. Owner will be able to stake with [stNEAR liquid staking pools (user stories Carol)], and to sell galdrastafir artworks as NFT cards for the game.

Staking ends Farming Starts

Auctioned NFT staves that were staked for one month will be automatically redeemed and may be traded in the [Paras Market], the APY for the staked investment will come from staking on the stNEAR tokenized stake Liquidity Pools. Users will have earned the right to contribute to the $meta DAO and will get 5 NFT Valkyrie cards each to start playing the game when it will be launched.
A farming Web3 interface will be launched to expand the players pool before the game is released.

Market Launch

The first NFT market launch will see the first set of cards for the Varda game, the Valkyries, they'll be legedary cards and will be exchanged and played in the game along with the new stave owners selected works.

The $META DAO project and why we choose them

The Varda game will use a particular wallet called Narwallet to be able to provide all the features of the Tokenized stake pool into the Varda game. Using this Social token players will contribute to the Liquidity pool while playing and so earn a passive income up to 7 times higher than the usual staking income. The art market will ensure selected content creators will get recognition from their art and players will be able to trade for playing.
We are committed to port our NFTs to the ethereum blockchain in an operational way, so that they can be exchanged and used as community assets over there too.
