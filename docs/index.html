the vision

Gamified NFT Marketplace

A niche NFTart marketplace, a gamified experience for investors.

Fouders Staves

The founders' staves have been studied on the icelandic historical symbols, in order to bring meaningful insights from the norse culture in a new, revisited way, to a new decentralised society. NFT standard NEP for interoperability with ethereum will be used. The auction of these NFT staves is the first step of our schedule and will enable contacts with early investors and interested artists that want to collaborate.

Staking start

Auctioned NFTs may be redeemed on the NEAR blockchain or staked for one month, the APY for the staked investment will be used as basic APY for future auctions on the marketplace.

Market Launch

The first NFT market launch will see the first set of cards for the Varda game, the Valkyries, they'll be legedary cards and will be exchanged and played in the game along with the new artists' selected works

Near Stakers collection

We are actively seeking for the best validators on the NEAR blockchain in order to ensure a stable earning to our art investors, APY will be transparent on each staking round. If you are an interested validator please get in touch below!

Rainbow bridge NFT exchange with ethereum

The card market/game launch will set the stage up for a more in depth collaboration with the rainbow bridge team. Then new artists will collaborate creating staves and gods cards, and we'll make sure their work will be visible and valuable on both NEAR and ethereum blockchain.

DeFi Game Launch

The DeFi integration will come with an Oracle integration for interoperability between blockchains, enabling artists to use their artworks on NEAR and ethereum and to set their price for both markets, staking on Near validators and DeFi pools, more info are coming.

Liquidity pools and oracles to establish the market price of Art

Liquidity pool is known as an investment that comes both with high risks and high earning, we aim to leverage its features to establish a price for each new artist that will join us after the market launch, helping them in getting recognised in both worlds, Ethereum and NEAR.

We are working to integrate the current oracles to our market for prediction and sale analytics.
